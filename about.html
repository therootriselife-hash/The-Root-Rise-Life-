Hello — I’m Shenelle

I created The Root & Rise Life because I wanted a gentle, soulful place to share the practices that helped me feel steady again. Becoming a mother taught me how tiny pauses — a written letter, a guided breath, a brief ritual — could change a day, a relationship, even a life.

Here I offer simple digital tools: Womb Letters, sacred pause kits, and small guides that meet you where you are. Everything I create is shaped by tenderness and an intention to hold space for your inner child and your present self.

What I value

  • Care: gentle, compassionate language and design.
  • Simplicity: tools that are easy to use and integrate into daily life.
  • Presence: offerings that invite you back to yourself.

If you want to connect, I’m here — whether for a question about a Womb Letter or to share how this work touched you.
